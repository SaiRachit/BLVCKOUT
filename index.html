From Signal to Recovery

Every reveal on the rail marks another stage in the system: service telemetry, anomaly normalization, root cause inference, and remediation readiness.

Telemetry Layer

Distributed services emit a continuous stream of clues.

API, auth, cache, database, payment, and notification services all produce structured logs and metrics, giving the platform a synchronized picture of live behavior.

Processing Layer

Event normalization transforms raw noise into usable state.

The event processor aligns timestamps, infers health, and builds a consistent operational timeline so RCA has a reliable substrate to reason over.

Inference Layer

Root cause analysis traces impact through the dependency chain.

Instead of stopping at symptoms, the system scores hypotheses, correlates degraded services, and highlights the most plausible initiating fault in seconds.

Control Layer

The dashboard becomes an operational cockpit, not just a readout.

Engineers can inspect incidents, trace journeys, watch SLA behavior, and inject controlled failures to validate resilience end to end.
